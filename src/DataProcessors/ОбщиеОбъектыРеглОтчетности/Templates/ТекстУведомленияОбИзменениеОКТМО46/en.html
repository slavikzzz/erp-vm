  • Zaraysk - 46729000 
  • Krasnogorsk - 46744000 
  • Lukhovitsy - 46747000 
  • Lyubertsy - 46748000 
  • Pavlovsky Posad - 46759000 
  • Ruza - 46766000 
  • Istra - 46733000 
  • Kolomensky - 46738000 
  • Naro-Fominsk - 46750000 
  • Stupino - 46776000 
  • Chekhov - 46784000 
  • Shatura - 46786000

If your company is registered in one of these municipal districts, please manually correct RNCMT codes in the registration directory of the tax authority.
