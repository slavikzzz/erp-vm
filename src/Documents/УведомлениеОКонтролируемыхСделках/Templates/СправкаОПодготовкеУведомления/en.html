Prepare a notification of controlled transactions
Preparation of the notification about controlled transactions consists of the following steps:
  • Entry of the information to fill the list of transactions
  • Filling the list of transactions on the grounds of the infobase data
  • Specification of the additional information for classification of transactions
  • Filling the notification about controlled transactions on the grounds of the complete list of transactions and the additional information
  • Review, control and editing of the notification
  • Printing the regulated form of the notification or uploading in a digital type
